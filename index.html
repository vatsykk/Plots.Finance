GAMERS

Levelling the playing field

The idea of co-owning assets and assigning a manager is not new in the physical world. However, it has not yet been applied in the virtual world, until now.
Plots aims to democratize virtual land ownership and make it accessible to all gamers, regardless of their financial status. This means that gamers who may have previously been excluded from owning virtual lands, can now join in and benefit from this exciting new asset class.

VLND minters provide the liquidity to co-own lands with gamers. All it takes is a simple ETH transfer to our smart contract, and investors can mint VLND tokens and become co-owners of virtual lands alongside active gamers.

Investors can now gain fungible co-ownership of virtual lands from collections that provide a sustainable yield produced by gamers levelling up.
